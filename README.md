## 🌟 About Me

Cloud consultant by day, climber / vinyl digger / amateur chef / occasional traveller by everything else. I've spent 10 years turning complex infrastructure problems into scalable cloud solutions — currently helping enterprises across Iberia adopt AWS.

I work with financial services, manufacturing, and retail customers on their cloud journey, from building solid foundations to designing architectures that actually scale. Before AWS, I worked on data-driven manufacturing solutions at Volkswagen Digital Solutions, and before that led cloud and DevOps at Banco Credibom — where I was also part of the founding team that launched Pisca Pisca, a used-vehicles platform. Earlier on, I did AI research on swarm robotics, co-founded a robotics control startup, and got my hands dirty with electronics for architectural and artistic installations.
